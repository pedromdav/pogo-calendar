Subscribe in Google Calendar

  1. Copy this feed URL:
    calendar.ics
  2. In Google Calendar → Other calendars (＋) → From URL.
  3. Paste the URL and click Add calendar.

On iPhone: Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar, then paste the same URL.
